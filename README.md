# Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This repository showcases a comprehensive **Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard** created using Power BI. The project analyzes regional sales, monthly trends, and customer purchase behavior to drive optimization in inventory and revenue strategy for the mobile retail brand 'Mobix'.

## 💡 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **Total Sales:** 769M
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 19K
* **Total Transactions:** 4K
* **Avg Sales Per Transaction:** 40K

### 🔍 Strategic Insights:
* **Geospatial Analysis:** Visualized sales distribution across major Indian cities, identifying high-demand hubs in the North and South regions.
* **Customer Sentiment:** Used a 'Count of Customer Ratings' funnel chart to identify that the majority of users fall in the 4-5 star rating bracket, reflecting high product satisfaction.
* **Payment Trends:** Analyzed payment methods to reveal an almost equal distribution between UPI, Debit, Cash, and Credit, indicating a diverse customer preference for payment modes.
* **Model Performance:** Identified Apple and OnePlus as top-selling brands, contributing significantly to the 769M total revenue.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
* **BI Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Processing:** Power Query (ETL) & DAX Measures
* **Visuals:** Heat Maps, Funnel Charts, Area Charts, and Trend Line Analysis
